Education

I have recieved my education at Codecademy. It is an online coding school that teaches students remotely through the completion of lessons quizzes and projects.
Their teaching model is to learn by doing, because every lesson requires the student to practice coding, and every project deepens the students understanding of both syntax and programming skill.
I have recieved two professional certificates from Codecademy, and they are Web Development and Front End Web Development. These certificates qualify me to work professionaly as a Front End Web Developer. It has taken me 10 months of intensive and dedicated study to earn these professional certificates.

Web Development Front End Web Development

I have completed 35 of their course certificates. I have studied 10 programming languages. I have learned C++, C#, Java, Java Script, HTML, CSS, SQL, PHP, Ruby, and Python.
I have studied both the basic and advanced techniques for each language, although the majority of my training has been dedicated to Front End Languages such as JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and PHP.
I am a dedicated self learner, I have and will continue to study programming in both syntax and theory. I am willing to learn any framework, and solve any problem that I experience.
